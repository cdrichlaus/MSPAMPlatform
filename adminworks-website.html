Engagement Snapshot

Mid-market MSP · Pacific Northwest · $3.8M revenue · 18 staff · Active peer group member

The Situation

After joining a peer group, the owner realized their P&L couldn't produce a benchmarkable gross margin. Revenue from managed services, cloud resale, and professional services was commingled, COGS had no consistent structure, and there was no framework aligned to how the industry measures performance.

What We Did

Started with a full GL audit to surface misallocations and categorization gaps. Rebuilt the QuickBooks chart of accounts to industry standards, separating managed services, cloud resale, and project revenue with corresponding COGS pairings. Established month-end close processes with deferred revenue recognition and payroll allocation. First benchmarkable P&L delivered within 60 days.
